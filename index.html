Who We Are

MARKETING FROM THE INSIDE

Most marketing agencies have never stepped in a batter's box. We have. ES Media Group was founded by a former D1 baseball player who understands the grind, the culture, and what it takes to compete at the highest level.

We translate that credibility into marketing that actually resonates with coaches, parents, and athletes — not just looks good on a slide deck.

Founder & CEO
Ethan Suh

Born and raised in New Jersey, Ethan made the jump across the country to attend Pepperdine University — where he walked on to the baseball program his sophomore year. He heard no more times than he can count. He kept showing up anyway until someone finally said yes.

That same mentality is the foundation of ES Media Group. Ethan built this agency to help programs, academies, and athletes get the brand recognition they deserve — because he knows firsthand what it feels like to bet on yourself when the odds aren't in your favor.
